How it works

  1. Claim reveal: AI drops a short claim from tech, science, or logic.
  2. Your call: trust or doubt with a tap and (optionally) a one-sentence reason.
  3. Validators chime in; appeal unlocks extra nodes if consensus differs.
  4. Score by matching validation or winning appeals; total XP and accuracy appear at round end.
